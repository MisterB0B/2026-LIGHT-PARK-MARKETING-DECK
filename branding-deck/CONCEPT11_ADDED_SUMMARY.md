## New Concept Added ✅

### Concept #11: The Grand Tent Entrance

**Slide Position**: 14 (between YouTube Channel and Expected Outcomes)

#### Description
An end-of-show attraction where Gnomies can get out and celebrate! This is the finale where guests can get out and dance, take photos with DJ Polar Ice, enjoy perfectly synced pixel mesh screen effects, and dance to Polar Ice's Arctic audio playlist—an immersive celebration that caps off THE LIGHT PARK experience with unforgettable moments and social media-worthy memories.

#### Key Features

**Entrance Features:**
- 5 × pixel screens (4' wide × 16' tall) positioned in front of every tent leg
- Large screens create stunning first impression and draw guests toward entrance
- Immersive content: welcoming animations, show previews, brand messaging
- Warm, inviting atmosphere to set the mood

**Interior Enhancements:**
- Custom-built concession display racks for optimal product presentation
- Modular design: can be broken down for easy storage and shipment
- Sales optimization through proper display presentation
- Clean, cohesive aesthetic throughout tent interior

#### Benefits
1. **Brand Consistency**: Implemented across all shows for consistent, professional look
2. **Sales Driver**: Better product displays increase concession revenue
3. **Portable Solution**: Modular design allows easy breakdown and shipment
4. **Guest Experience**: Creates excitement and anticipation from arrival

#### Venue-Wide Implementation
- Unified brand identity across all locations
- Professional, polished appearance at every venue
- Scalable solution that grows with the brand
- Cost-effective through standardized design and manufacturing
