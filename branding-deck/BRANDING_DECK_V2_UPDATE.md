### 3. Cost Reference Removal

#### Removed All Dollar Amounts
- Timeline investment amounts ($5,000, $3,000, etc.)

- Total investment figures ($38,000, $50,000, $88,500)
- Revenue projections ($1.5M-$2M, $500K-$1M, etc.)
- Cost comparisons ($5,000-$15,000 vs $100-$500)
- Savings calculations ($490K-$1.45M)
- Book pricing ($15-$20)
- Estimated costs for concepts

#### Updated to Qualitative Language

**Before:**

## Design Philosophy

### Neon Aesthetic
The new design embraces a modern, tech-forward neon aesthetic that:
- Creates visual impact with high contrast
- Reinforces THE LIGHT PARK brand identity
- Matches the playbook's visual language
- Provides professional, premium feel
- Enhances readability with strategic glow effects

### Focus on Value, Not Cost
The updated content emphasizes:
- Strategic partnerships over transactions
- Value creation over expenditure
- Long-term ROI over short-term costs
- Qualitative benefits over quantitative metrics
- Partnership capabilities over pricing
