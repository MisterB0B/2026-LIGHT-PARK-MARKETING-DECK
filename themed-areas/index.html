THE LIGHT PARK
THEMED AREAS

Experience 25 immersive themed areas featuring spectacular light displays, interactive elements, and unforgettable holiday magic. From DJ Polar Ice's Mix Lab to Pixel Peaks Canopy, each area offers a unique journey through a winter wonderland of lights and sound.

Area #1

Barnyard Beat

A festive tunnel featuring farm animal light displays synced to upbeat holiday music, creating a barnyard dance party atmosphere.

Area #2

Pixel Plaza

Main street entrance with pixel art Christmas trees and digital light displays, setting the tone for the modern light experience.

Area #3

Frozen Fortress

An icy wonderland with blue and white lights creating a magical frozen castle atmosphere with snowflake projections.

Area #4

Candy Cane Corner

A sweet section featuring giant candy cane arches and peppermint-themed decorations in red and white spirals.

Area #5

TLP Express Station

A vintage train station scene with animated light trains and holiday travelers, complete with steam effects.

Area #6

Morning Glory

A sunrise-themed area with warm golden lights transitioning through dawn colors, creating a peaceful morning atmosphere.

Area #7

Pixel Peaks Passage

Mountain-themed tunnel with pixel mesh creating animated snow-capped peaks and northern lights effects.

Area #8

Lone Star Lights

Texas-themed section with cowboy boots, stars, and western holiday decorations celebrating local heritage.

Area #9

Gnome Grotto Grove

Whimsical garden featuring animated gnome characters and mushroom houses with twinkling fairy lights.

Area #10

Rings of Radiance

Circular light tunnels creating mesmerizing patterns and color transitions as guests drive through.

Area #11

Melody Mile

Musical note-shaped lights dancing to synchronized holiday tunes, creating a visual symphony.

Area #12

Ornament Opera

Giant ornament displays with intricate patterns and colors, celebrating classic Christmas decorations.

Area #13

Honor & Heroes Lane

Patriotic tribute with red, white, and blue lights honoring service members and first responders.

Area #14

Toyland Tinsel Trail

Classic toy-themed displays with teddy bears, toy soldiers, and jack-in-the-boxes brought to life with lights.

Area #15

Santa's Skyway

Overhead light display featuring Santa's sleigh and reindeer flying across the night sky.

Area #16

DJ Polar Ice's Mix Lab

Interactive DJ booth with synchronized light shows and music mixing displays featuring the park's mascot.

Area #17

Snowflake Symphony

Delicate snowflake patterns projected and illuminated, creating a serene winter wonderland atmosphere.

Area #18

Electric Elves Dance Party

High-energy section with animated elf characters dancing to upbeat holiday remixes with strobe effects.

Area #19

Pixel Peaks Canopy

Overhead pixel mesh creating a digital sky with animated stars, auroras, and holiday scenes.

Area #20

Candlelight Crescendo

Elegant candle-themed displays with warm flickering lights creating a peaceful, traditional atmosphere.

Area #21

Wreath Way Arches

Series of illuminated wreath arches creating a tunnel of traditional holiday greenery and lights.

Area #22

Holly Berry Boulevard

Red and green themed pathway with holly leaf patterns and berry clusters illuminated along the route.

Area #23

Elf Avenue

Whimsical elf workshop scene with animated elves building toys and preparing for Christmas.

Area #24

Noble Nutcrackers Gateway

Grand entrance featuring towering nutcracker soldiers standing guard with synchronized light movements.

Area #25

Snowman Serenade Street

Cheerful snowman family displays with animated singing and dancing snowmen spreading holiday joy.
